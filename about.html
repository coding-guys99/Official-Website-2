About KeySearch

Find it. Fast. No mess. Just answers.

Mission

Help people find exactly what they need—across notes, files, and ideas—instantly, without clutter, lock-in, or heavy cloud dependencies.

Local-first

Your data lives on your device (IndexedDB). Works offline. No account required.

Fast by design

Indexed search with tags, filters, and global shortcuts—measured in milliseconds.

No lock-in

Export to CSV/JSON. Share read-only collections when you need to collaborate.

Multi-language

Switch UI instantly with i18n JSON. Ready for 17+ locales and counting.

Privacy & Transparency

KeySearch is privacy-first. We don’t sell data. Optional telemetry is off by default and can be disabled anytime. Clear exports are built-in.
